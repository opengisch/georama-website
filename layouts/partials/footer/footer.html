About Georama

Georama is an open-source web publishing platform for the QGIS ecosystem, designed to streamline the deployment and serving of geospatial data through modern, standards-based web services. It enables organisations to publish, manage, and scale geodata workflows while retaining full control over permissions, infrastructure, and data ownership.

Open-source

Georama will be released as open-source software. This guarantees transparency, long-term sustainability, and the freedom to inspect, adapt, and extend the platform to fit your own infrastructure and workflows.
