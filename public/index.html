The most powerful and efficient way to manage and publish your data

Georama is a developer-first framework for managing and publishing spatial data. Whether you're running environmental assessments, urban planning tools, or community mapping projects, Georama gives you the tools to scale your operations without reinventing the wheel.

Unified Integration

Combine WMS, WFS, and QGIS data into one seamless platform. Georama harmonizes your geospatial stack without custom glue code.

Modular Architecture

Build your system your way. Each Georama module is fully decoupled and API-first â€” ready to scale with your needs.

Integrated WebGIS

Visualize published layers instantly in the browser. Our built-in viewer is fast, clean, and ready for both public and private maps.

Granular Permissions

Control access by user, group, or dataset. Georama lets you publish confidently with baked-in, role-aware security.

OGC-Compliant APIs

Built on standards: OAPIF, WMS 1.3.0, GeoJSON. Georama plays well with QGIS, OpenLayers, and any modern GIS stack.

Dev-Ready & Documented

Dockerized deployment, clean code, and full documentation make Georama ideal for teams building production tools.
